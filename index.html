Our Education Programs

Compare Programs
Mobymax

MobyMax is an online learning platform that provides engaging instruction in Math, English, Science, and Social Studies for homeschool students in grades K-9. With interactive, gamified lessons that adapt to each student’s pace, MobyMax helps reinforce skills and build a solid academic foundation across core subjects.

Study Island

Study Island is an online learning platform designed for homeschool students in grades 4-9, covering key subjects like Math, English, Science, and Social Studies. With a mix of interactive and workbook-style lessons with printable worksheets, Study Island allows students to master core concepts at their own pace, helping them achieve strong academic outcomes through engaging, effective practice.

Wolkskool

Wolkskool is an online learning platform developed to deliver quality Afrikaans education. Designed for students from grades 1 to 12, it offers a comprehensive curriculum aligned with South Africa’s CAPS standards. Through video lessons, assessments, and interactive workbooks, Wolkskool enables students to learn at their own pace, fostering independent study. This platform supports blended learning with a broad range of subjects and is an ideal tool for parents and educators seeking to enhance student outcomes through accessible, modern methods.

TABE

TABE (Test of Adult Basic Education) is a pre-GED assessment platform designed to support adult learners in building foundational skills. Covering core subjects like reading, math, and language, TABE aligns with adult education standards to help learners progress toward their educational and career goals. Through comprehensive assessments and targeted practice, TABE provides educators and learners with clear insights into skill levels and areas for growth. Ideal for both independent learning and instructor-led environments, TABE enables adults to learn at their own pace, promoting confidence and success in both academic and workplace settings.

GED

GED (General Educational Development) is an education and testing platform that enables adult learners to earn a high school equivalency diploma. Covering essential subjects like math, science, social studies, and language arts, GED aligns with high school curriculum standards, helping students prepare for college or career advancement. Through practice tests, study guides, and interactive lessons, GED supports flexible, self-paced learning to fit individual schedules and learning styles. Ideal for those seeking a high school credential, GED equips students with the knowledge and skills to succeed academically and professionally.

M&M

Mentoring and Monitoring was created to assist parents in assuring their child's online learning success. This service connects your child with a dedicated tutor via WhatsApp, offering support through calls or messages whenever needed. The tutor conducts regular checkups to ensure your child remains on track with their studies. Mentoring and Monitoring is perfect for parents who may not have the time for regular checkups or to familiarize themselves with MobyMax’s parental interface.
